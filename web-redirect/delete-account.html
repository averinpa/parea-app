Delete your Parea account

Last updated: 17 June 2026

How to delete your account

You can delete your Parea account and all associated personal data directly from inside the app:

  1. Open the Parea app on your device.
  2. Sign in to the account you want to delete (if not already signed in).
  3. Go to the Profile tab (bottom right).
  4. Tap the Settings gear icon at the top of the Profile screen.
  5. Scroll to the Account section.
  6. Tap Delete Account.
  7. Confirm the deletion in the dialog.

Your account is deleted immediately and your data is removed from our active systems within 24 hours.

If you cannot access the app

If you have lost access to the app (uninstalled, locked out, or otherwise unable to sign in), you can request deletion by emailing us:

Email: privacy@joinparea.app

Please send the request from the email address linked to your Parea account so we can verify ownership. We will confirm deletion within 7 days.

What we delete

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your profile (name, age, gender, photos, bio, languages, interests, vibe)
  • Your city and location preferences
  • Your event history (events you joined or hosted)
  • Your chat messages — both messages you sent and messages others sent to you in your chats
  • Your crew memberships
  • Your push notification token
  • Your authentication account (email / Google / Apple link)

What we keep, and why

To comply with our legal obligations, prevent abuse, and protect other users, we retain a small amount of information after account deletion:

WhatWhyRetention
Anonymised audit logs (e.g. that an account was deleted at a date)Audit / compliance under GDPR Art. 5(2)2 years
Block / report records you filedTo protect other users from abuse, even if you delete your own account2 years (then anonymised)
Aggregated usage statistics (no personally identifying fields)Service improvementIndefinite, anonymous

These retained records do not contain your name, email, photos, chat content, or any other identifying personal data.

Note: Account deletion is permanent. We cannot restore a deleted account or its data. If you only want to stop receiving notifications or take a break, you can sign out of the app without deleting your account.

If you have any questions about account deletion, what happens to your data, or your rights under GDPR, contact us:

  • Email: privacy@joinparea.app
