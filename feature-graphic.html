How to save as PNG (Chrome, exact 1024×500):
1. Press F12 to open DevTools
2. Press Ctrl+Shift+M to toggle device toolbar
3. Set viewport size to 1024 × 500 in the top bar
4. Click the three-dot menu (top-right of device toolbar) → Capture screenshot
5. Saved PNG will be exactly 1024×500 — upload to Play Console
